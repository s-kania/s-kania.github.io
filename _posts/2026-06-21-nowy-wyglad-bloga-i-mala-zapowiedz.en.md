The blog has had a bit of a visual refresh. For a long time I wanted it to feel more like its own thing and fit better with what I want to post here more often: posts about making games.

The biggest change is on the home page. There are new post cards, a side panel with categories, a mini browser with links, and a rougher, more computer-like vibe. It is still supposed to be simple, just less generic.

I also fixed a few small things for reading. Posts should have a bit more breathing room, photos and text should not fight each other, and the whole thing should work better on a phone. Something will probably still come out in the wash, because it always does, but the base is much closer to what I had in my head.

And the most important bit: updates from the game are coming back soon. I have a few things to show and a couple of topics worth writing about separately, instead of cramming everything into one huge status update. I want to get back to shorter devlogs, but more often.

That's it for today.
