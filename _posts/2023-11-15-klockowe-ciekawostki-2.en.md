LEGO® has just treated fans of classic Castle sets in a particularly nasty way. For half a year there were rumors and teasers about a mysterious gift-with-purchase set. Every one of us was hoping for something as good as last year's [**40567 Forest Hideout**](https://www.bricklink.com/v2/catalog/catalogitem.page?S=40567-1).

And then the [**40601 Majisto's Magical Workshop**](https://www.lego.com/pl-pl/product/majisto-s-magical-workshop-40601) set appeared before our eyes.

It turned out that the final product not only looks **bad**, but to get it, you will have to spend as much as **1200 PLN** in the LS store! Early rumors talked about an entry threshold of 1100 PLN. As a reminder, to get the Forest Hideout, you had to spend "only" 690 PLN. In January we got a remake of the Blacktron ship, set **[40580](https://www.bricklink.com/v2/catalog/catalogitem.page?S=40580-1)**, where the minimum spend was 900 PLN.

As you can see, LEGO® is testing the limits of our nostalgia. If only they had given the knight a printed shield and a special helmet. Sadly, in its current state I rate this set as **junk**. You are much better off getting a used original, although that is not cheap either: the average price of used models is 312 PLN.

![Set 6048](/assets/posts/2023-11-15-klockowe-ciekawostki-2/1700164703622.png "6048")
_Set 6048. Photo from [BrickLink](https://www.bricklink.com/v2/catalog/catalogitem.page?S=6048-1)_

If someone still has an appetite for this new release, it will be available on 18-19.11.2023. There will also be double Insiders points and some other nonsense, but that is not today's topic. I suspect, though, that many people will skip **[40601](https://www.lego.com/pl-pl/product/majisto-s-magical-workshop-40601)** and it will show up in a similar promo at least a couple more times.
