A few days ago I visited Czchów Castle, took my Black Falcon minifigures with me, and gave them a photo session. I also added a few photos of siege engines, maybe it will inspire someone to build a MOC 😉

This stronghold is located in Lesser Poland and, besides the main attraction, also offers a World War I and World War II park/trail with many open-air exhibits.
![Czchów Castle tower](/assets/posts/2023-05-11/IMG_4070.JPG){: width="972" height="589" }
If you are interested in the history of this charming castle, be sure to visit this page ->
<a href="https://zamkomania.pl/czchow.php" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Zamkomania - Czchów</a>
